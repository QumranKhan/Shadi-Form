Welcome to the FAQ's page

We have tried to answer the most frequently asked questions. If you cannot find what you are looking for, please feel free to contact us.

Why should I register on Meri Shadi?
We are an INTRODUCTORY and FREE service, aimed towards British-born Pakistani families.
How do I register?
The first step is to fill out the Marriage Candidate Form on the homepage.
How long will it take to register?
The questions are very simple so give yourself around 15-20 minutes to complete the form comprehensively.
Can I register for my friend, relative or sibling?
This service is aimed towards parents of british-born Pakistani children who are looking for a suitable match but are having trouble getting started. While completing the form, you will notice there is a section which asks for your relationship to the marriage candidate if you are not an immediate relative.
This rishta form seems very lengthy, why I am being asked so much information?
Like you, others will also be completing this form as comprehensively as possible. This is so you are able to get a full, rounded picture of the person who the profile is has been completed for.
What kind of information should I provide in my Rishta Form?
The form is simple and easy to follow. You will be asked information about yourself (if you are the marriage candidate) and then partner requirements.
Why should I provide information/demand about my expected partner?
There are sections dedicated to partner requirements. It is important that you complete this section as fully as you can. This will give potential matches a good idea about the kind of match that you are looking for.
Is my contact information public?
Ee are an introductory service, providing the first step in matchmaking. Your information is released at your responsibility and will ONLY be used for the sole purpose of providing a free, introductory profile search to potential matches.
How can I get more response to my profile?
The more comprehensive your profile is, the more likely it is to be viewed.
Can I edit, stop or delete my rishta profile?
Yes, contact us if you wish to edit or delete your profile. Please keep you ID number (assigned to each profile) in a safe place.
I am trying to register but I get an error that this email address already has an account, what does it mean?
This means that the email address you are trying to use already has a linked account. Please use a different email address.
